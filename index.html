Welcome to Ksd (Pty) Ltd - Empowering, Growth, Transforming Lives!


KHUVUTLU SKILLS DEVELOPMENT PTY LTD (Ksd) is 100% black owned company established in 2014 after research done on economic growth and poverty alleviation in South Africa. The founder is Mr Simon Makondo a qualified Master Installation Electrician, Electrical Line Mechanics Artisan and Occupational Directed Education Training Development Practitioner (OD-ETDP) National Diploma.


Ksd is a South African business located in the Northern part of the country in Limpopo Province and a head office is at the center of the Province in Polokwane.


Ksd main business is in the Energy sector- Electrical industry and focused on the Distribution power system up to 132kV network, Electrical construction and Training. KSD also took an expansion into small scale farming as a compulsory food security business in the province.
Ksd intend and have some development plans to retain and transfer scarce, core and critical skills technically both nationally and globally with business improvement strategy and a clear commitment towards a viable and new approach to poverty alleviation.
